INITIATIVES

To fulfill our vision to build an ecosystem where networking and entrepreneurial mindset are encouraged, we have conceptualized and are successfully running the following initiatives to help future entrepreneurs in their entrepreneurial journey.

Enterpreneurship

Programmes

Clubs/Camps

  • Rural Enterpreneurship
    An initiative where we promote the potential and creative entrepreneurial ideas from various sections of society and present them to the vast network of entrepreneurs and investors, spotlighting the unique talents.
  • Alumnus Enterpreneurship
    An initiative by E-CELL in association with Alumni Association that organizes StartUp Master sessions that are highly enriching and enlightening for entrepreneurs which are graced by many famous personalities.
  • E-mentoring Program
    E-Mentoring Program is an initiative of E-CELL RGPV helping which helps budding ventures turn their dream into reality, under this program we provide assistance and guidance to water blooming startup ideas.
  • Startup Internship Program
    Startup Internship Program (SIP) strives for developing an entrepreneurial streak among campus students by helping them acquire an internship in the most spirited and exciting startups running around.
  • Campus Ambassador Program
    The Campus Ambassador Program is an initiative by E-Cell RGPV to reach out to all the colleges across India intended to incorporate entrepreneurial spirit in students and foster an entrepreneurial mindset.
  • Startup Incubation
    An initiative by E-cell RGPV that caters to the growing startups of our country. Sharing of entrepreneurship endeavors and experiences by experts provides profuse guidance to future entrepreneurs and technopreneurs.
  • Enterpreneurship Awareness Camp
    An initiative that provides the enthusiasts with information about entrepreneurship culture within different campuses and breaking the ice of unawareness between the potential entrepreneurs and their success.
